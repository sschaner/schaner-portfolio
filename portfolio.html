Point of Service Console App

I collaborated with a team of fellow Grand Circus students to develop a point of service console app. The app reads and writes from a text file, displays the products, allows the user to choose which products they wish to buy, and adds them to a cart to buy with their chosen payment method. An employee has the ability to add products to the text file database.
